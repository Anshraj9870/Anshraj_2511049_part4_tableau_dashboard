# Business Insights

## Insight 1: Sales Trend

**Observation:**
The sales trend shows an overall increasing pattern during the selected time period.

**Data Evidence:**
Sales reached the highest value in **August 2025** and the lowest value in **May 2025**.

**Business Interpretation:**
The business experiences seasonal demand, with stronger sales during certain months.

**Recommended Action:**
Increase inventory, marketing campaigns, and staffing before peak sales periods to maximize revenue.

---

## Insight 2: Regional Performance

**Observation:**
The **South** region generated the highest sales, while the **East** region recorded the lowest sales.

**Data Evidence:**
Highest Sales Region: **South**

Lowest Sales Region: **East**

**Business Interpretation:**
The South region has stronger customer demand and market penetration compared to the East region.

**Recommended Action:**
Study the successful sales strategy used in the South region and implement similar initiatives in the East region.

---

## Insight 3: Category Profitability

**Observation:**
The **Technology** category, especially **Machines**, generated the highest profit, while the **Furniture** category, particularly **Tables**, produced the lowest profit.

**Data Evidence:**
Highest Profit Category: **Technology**

Lowest Profit Category: **Furniture**

**Business Interpretation:**
Some product categories contribute significantly to profitability, whereas others reduce overall business performance.

**Recommended Action:**
Increase focus on profitable categories and review pricing, procurement costs, and product strategy for low-profit categories.

---

## Insight 4: Customer Segment Performance

**Observation:**
The **Home Office** segment contributed the highest sales and profit.

**Data Evidence:**
Top Customer Segment: **Home Office**

**Business Interpretation:**
Home Office customers represent the company's most valuable customer group.

**Recommended Action:**
Launch loyalty programs and targeted marketing campaigns to retain and grow this customer segment.

---

## Insight 5: Discount Impact

**Observation:**
Higher discounts generally resulted in lower profits.

**Data Evidence:**
The scatter plot indicates a negative relationship between discount percentage and profit.

**Business Interpretation:**
Offering excessive discounts reduces profit margins and may negatively affect overall profitability.

**Recommended Action:**
Review the discount policy and avoid large discounts on products with already low profit margins.

---

## Insight 6: Shipping Performance

**Observation:**
The **Same Day** shipping mode has the lowest average delivery time, while **Standard Class** has the highest average delivery time.

**Data Evidence:**
The Shipping Performance view shows that **Same Day** recorded the lowest average delivery days, whereas **Standard Class** recorded the highest average delivery days.

**Business Interpretation:**
Faster delivery services improve customer satisfaction and provide a better customer experience. Standard Class shipments take longer and may affect customer expectations if delivery times are not managed properly.

**Recommended Action:**
Promote Same Day shipping for urgent customer orders and improve the logistics efficiency of Standard Class deliveries to reduce overall delivery time.

---

## Insight 7: Return Analysis

**Observation:**
The **Furniture** category recorded the highest number of returned orders.

**Data Evidence:**
Return analysis shows that Furniture has the highest return volume.

**Business Interpretation:**
High return rates may indicate product quality issues, customer expectation gaps, or delivery-related problems.

**Recommended Action:**
Analyze return reasons, improve product quality, and provide more accurate product information to customers.

---

## Insight 8: Business Opportunity and Risk

**Observation:**
High-performing regions and customer segments create growth opportunities, while high discounts and high-return categories present business risks.

**Data Evidence:**
Dashboard KPIs and visualizations highlight strong regional performance, profitable categories, and areas with higher return rates.

**Business Interpretation:**
Improving weak-performing regions while strengthening successful business areas can significantly improve overall profitability.

**Recommended Action:**
Expand successful business strategies to underperforming regions, optimize discount policies, reduce returns, and continue investing in profitable product categories.
