# Executive Dashboard Story

# Executive Summary

This executive dashboard provides a comprehensive overview of the company's sales performance, profitability, customer segments, regional performance, shipping efficiency, discount impact, and product returns. The dashboard is designed to help leadership monitor business performance, identify operational risks, and make data-driven strategic decisions.

---

# What is Performing Well

The business demonstrates positive sales growth over the selected period, with the highest sales recorded in **August 2025**. The **South** region is the strongest-performing region in terms of sales, indicating strong customer demand and effective market penetration.

The **Technology** category, particularly **Machines**, contributes the highest profit, making it one of the most valuable product groups. In addition, the **Home Office** customer segment generates the highest sales and profit, highlighting it as an important customer segment for future business growth.

Shipping performance also shows that **Same Day** shipping provides the fastest delivery service, helping improve customer satisfaction for urgent orders.

---

# What is Underperforming

The **East** region generates the lowest sales and requires additional attention. The **Furniture** category, especially **Tables**, produces the lowest profitability, reducing overall business performance.

The dashboard also shows that **Standard Class** shipping has the longest delivery time compared to other shipping modes, which may affect customer satisfaction.

Furthermore, the return analysis indicates that the **Furniture** category has the highest number of returned products, suggesting possible issues related to product quality, customer expectations, or delivery.

---

# Risks Identified

The dashboard highlights several business risks.

High discount levels are associated with lower profitability, indicating that excessive discounting reduces profit margins.

The high return rate in the Furniture category may increase operational costs and reduce customer confidence.

Long delivery times for Standard Class shipments could negatively impact the customer experience if not improved.

---

# Business Opportunities

The dashboard identifies multiple opportunities for business growth.

The company can increase investment in the Technology category because it generates strong profits.

Marketing campaigns targeting the Home Office customer segment may further increase revenue.

Successful sales strategies from the South region can be replicated in lower-performing regions such as the East.

Improving Standard Class delivery efficiency and reducing return rates can further improve customer satisfaction and profitability.

---

# Recommended Business Actions

- Expand successful sales strategies from the South region into lower-performing regions.
- Increase inventory and promotional focus on Technology products.
- Review pricing and cost strategies for the Furniture category.
- Optimize the discount policy to protect profit margins.
- Improve Standard Class delivery performance.
- Investigate the reasons for high product returns in the Furniture category.
- Continue investing in the Home Office customer segment through loyalty and retention programs.

---

# Dashboard Limitations

The dashboard is based only on the available historical sales dataset and does not include external factors such as competitor activity, customer feedback, marketing expenditure, or seasonal events.

The analysis is descriptive and identifies trends and patterns but does not predict future business performance.
